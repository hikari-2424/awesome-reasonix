# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Our Standards

**Positive behavior:**
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community

**Unacceptable behavior:**
- Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Submitting malicious or intentionally broken Skills
- Spamming or submitting low-quality entries repeatedly
- Manipulating ratings through fake reviews

## Enforcement

Community leaders are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards. Violations may result in:

1. **Warning** — For first-time, minor infractions.
2. **Temporary ban** — For repeated or moderate violations.
3. **Permanent ban** — For severe or persistent violations.

## Reporting

Report violations by opening a [flag issue](https://github.com/hikari-2424/awesome-reasonix/issues/new?template=skill-flag.yml) or contacting the maintainers.
