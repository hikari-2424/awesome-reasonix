# learn-topic

Research a topic and output structured notes.

## Flow

1. Search — use web_search for 3-5 quality sources
2. Fetch — use web_fetch to read 2-3 most valuable articles
3. Organize — output structured notes under 500 words
4. Flag information certainty (confirmed / likely / unverified)
